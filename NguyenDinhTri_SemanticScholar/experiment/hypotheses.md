### **Statistical Hypotheses for GPT-4o BDD Automation** 1

This document outlines the formal hypotheses used to evaluate **GPT-4o zero-shot (temperature=0)** performance when generating BDD scenarios from user stories in the Connextra format 1\. The goal of the experiment is to verify if the model meets specific benchmarks for both structural integrity and semantic alignment 1\.

#### **RQ1 — Semantic Similarity** 1, 2

**Goal:** To determine if the average cosine semantic similarity, measured via the **all-MiniLM-L6-v2** model, between GPT-4o generated scenarios and expert-written benchmarks exceeds a **0.85** threshold 2\.

* **Null Hypothesis (H0\_1):** average\_similarity \<= 0.85 2\.  
* *Interpretation:* GPT-4o has not reached the required threshold for semantic accuracy 2\.  
* **Alternative Hypothesis (H1\_1):** average\_similarity \> 0.85 2\.  
* *Interpretation:* GPT-4o successfully reaches the required threshold for semantic accuracy 2\.  
* **Statistical Test:** Wilcoxon signed-rank test 2\.  
* **Significance Level:** alpha \= 0.05 2\.

#### **RQ2 — Executable Syntax** 3

**Goal:** To determine if the rate of syntactically correct (structurally valid) Gherkin scenarios generated by GPT-4o exceeds the **80%** benchmark 3\.

* **Null Hypothesis (H0\_2):** executable\_rate \<= 0.80 3\.  
* *Interpretation:* The generated scenarios do not meet the minimum structural validity requirement 3\.  
* **Alternative Hypothesis (H1\_2):** executable\_rate \> 0.80 3\.  
* *Interpretation:* The generated scenarios significantly exceed the minimum structural validity requirement 3\.  
* **Statistical Test:** Binomial test (one-tailed, p0 \= 0.80) 3\.  
* **Significance Level:** alpha \= 0.05 3\.

#### **Decision Rule** 4

The experimental intervention is considered a success only if **both null hypotheses** (H0\_1 and H0\_2) are rejected 4\. A successful rejection confirms that the zero-shot performance of GPT-4o is statistically significant regarding its reliability in producing executable code and its ability to capture business intent 4\.
